Numerical Simulation of Quantum Materials

We use numerical simulation softwares such as FDTD simulations, Matlab, Comsol to study the physical properties, e.g. transimision, reflection, thermal radiation etc. We use the simulation result to optimize the fabrication of the nanophotonic devices based on quantum materials.
