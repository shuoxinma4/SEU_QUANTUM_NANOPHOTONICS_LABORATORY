Postdoctoral positions

Applications and nominations are welcome for a postdoctoral research position in the field of experimental condensed matter physics in the Kim group. Applicants should submit a curriculum vitae, statement of research interests, and arrange letters of reference. The materials should be emailed to: yueluo@seu.edu.cn. Recommendation letters from senior scientists should also be emailed to this address. Review of applications will begin immediately.

Master and Doctoral students

We have several Master and Doctoral student positions every year. Applicants should submit a curriculum vitae and transcript. The materials should be emailed to: yueluo@seu.edu.cn.
